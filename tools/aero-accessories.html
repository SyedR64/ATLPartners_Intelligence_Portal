Methodology: 150 dual-certified Part 145 repair stations (FAA Part 145 + EASA Part-145 via BASA) located in the United States. Universe sourced from the public list of foreign Part-145 organisations the EASA maintains for dual-cert compliance (easa.europa.eu), which is the cleanest free public source for US shops carrying both certifications. Real fields: company name, FAA certificate number, street, city, state, EASA approval & renewal date. Illustrative overlays: FAA ratings (derived from name keywords — A/I/D/H/P/E), employee counts, ownership class.
